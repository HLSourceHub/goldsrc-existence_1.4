Here is the current list of existence cvars. These are for greater control over game elements on a server.

mp_roundlimit = the number of maximum rounds that can be played on a map. When the limit is hit the map will change to the next in the mapcycle.txt. Example mp_roundlimit 5 will let only 5 rounds happen. When the 5th round ends the map will change.

mp_restarttime = Controls the amount of time to wait inbetween rounds before starting a new one. It allows people to have time to change teams.

mp_jailtime = The time white has to be rescued. Setting this will increase or decrease the amount of time the rebels have to rescue dr. white
