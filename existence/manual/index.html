Existence is an objective based, action modification for Valves Half-Life. In it there are 2 teams, the rebel force, headed up by a Dr Justin White, a defector of the second team, the Blacklight Troops, assembled by the Neurotek Coporation to combat the terror like actions of the rebel force.

Neurotek is a mega corporation, dealing with various projects and bought out by the Government due it's advanced research and high-tec technology. But it's all got out of hand and the system has become too powerful, it must be stopped. Thats what the rebel force are out to do. They must locate the sites and destroy them so they cannot be recovered. The Black Light troops must protect the sites and capture Dr White so his knowledge can be analysed and the rebel stronghold be infiltrated and annihilated.

In essence the mod is attack vs defence. The rebels have to plant the c4 at a designated spot on a map and the agents have to capture Dr White by killing him, where he'll be held in a cell for 3 minutes. After 3 minutes the agent team wins. The rebels have to free Dr White within those 3 minutes and escape through a phonebooth, making sure the hardline switch is activated, otherwise he wont be able to teleport.

For more information about the mod, take a look at the site and browse around the forums. Report any bugs to the bugs forum. Enjoy the mod!
